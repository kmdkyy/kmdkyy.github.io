通过laptop-mode-tools控制debian屏幕亮度

2021年8月27日 10:49:19

CONTROL_BRIGHTNESS=1

BATT_BRIGHTNESS_COMMAND="echo 13"
LM_AC_BRIGHTNESS_COMMAND="echo 13"
NOLM_AC_BRIGHTNESS_COMMAND="echo 13"
BRIGHTNESS_OUTPUT="/sys/class/backlight/acpi_video0/brightness"

步骤

  1. 安装laptop-mode-tools
  2. 所有设置请man laptop-mode.conf.
  3. 查看这个文件：/etc/laptop-mode/conf.d/lcd-brightness.conf
